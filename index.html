How it works

This app is here to help you to get weather forecast for a journey with a complex route. Think Road Trip where you stay at a different location each day.

Simply pick the start date of your journey and its duration. Enter planned stops for each day of your journey and get weather for this particular stop on a given day.

Weather forecast is powered by Dark Sky.
